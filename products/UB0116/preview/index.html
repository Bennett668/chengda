Inside the bowl

A bowl that sheds water on its own.

The slanted base is not a stylistic choice. It is a working slope — three degrees from rim to drain — that pulls water toward the centre when the tap closes. No standing pool. No wipe-down. The basin reads as flat from above; reveals its slope from the side.
